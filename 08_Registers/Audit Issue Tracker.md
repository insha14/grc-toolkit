# Audit Issue Tracker

## Overview
The Audit Issue Tracker maintains a record of all audit findings identified during internal or external audits. It helps ensure accountability, timely remediation, and proper documentation for future audits and compliance assessments.

## Purpose
This tracker provides visibility into open issues, responsible owners, remediation progress, deadlines, and final verification. It supports continuous improvement and strengthens the organization’s control environment.

## Notes
- High-severity issues should be reviewed more frequently.  
- “Status” should reflect Open, In Progress, Completed, or Reopened.  
- Evidence of remediation must be retained for audit cycles.  
- Review this tracker monthly or after major assessments.
