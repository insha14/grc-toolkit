# Audit Finding Template

## Overview
The Audit Finding Template is used to document observations identified during internal audits, external audits, or compliance assessments. It provides a standardized structure to capture the nature of the finding, its risk implications, and corrective action plans.

## Purpose
This template ensures consistency in how findings are reported, prioritized, and remediated. It also supports transparency with management and provides evidence for future audits.
